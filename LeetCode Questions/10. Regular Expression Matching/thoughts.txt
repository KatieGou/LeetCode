Hard question! Learnt about re (Regular expression operations) and its functions. 

'.' matches any chars. '^' matches the start of the string. '$' matches the end of the string. '*' matches 0 or more repetition (greedy). '+' matches 1 or more repetition (greedy). '?' matches 0 or 1 repetition (greedy). 

'*?', '+?', '??': non-greedy version of '*', '+' and '?': match as fewer chars as possible.
